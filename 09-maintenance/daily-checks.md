# Daily Checks (on passage / in use)

- **Category:** Maintenance
- **Style:** DO–CONFIRM
- **When to use:** Each day the boat is in use, ideally each morning.
- **Crew:** Skipper or delegated.

> Killer items are shown in **bold**.

## Engine & systems
- [ ] Engine oil and coolant levels
- [ ] **Bilge dry; check for new water/oil/fuel**
- [ ] Belt condition; look for leaks around the engine
- [ ] Battery state of charge; charging working
- [ ] Fuel and water tank levels; plan for refills

## Deck & rig
- [ ] Walk the deck: **rigging, fittings, split pins, shackles all secure**
- [ ] Halyards/sheets for chafe; ease/re-lead as needed
- [ ] Sails for damage or chafe at spreaders/stanchions
- [ ] Fenders/warps stowed; nothing dragging

## Safety
- [ ] Lifejackets/harnesses/tethers in good order
- [ ] Jackstays sound (if rigged)
- [ ] Navigation lights working
- [ ] Fire extinguishers/first-aid accessible

## Admin
- [ ] Weather update obtained
- [ ] Log up to date; position fixed
- [ ] Water/food/fuel adequate for the day

## Notes & gotchas
- A daily rig walk catches small failures (a working split pin, a chafing halyard)
  before they become dismastings or breakages.
