# Bilge & Pump Test

- **Category:** Supplementary
- **Style:** DO–CONFIRM
- **When to use:** Part of daily checks and before a passage.
- **Crew:** Anyone.

> Killer items are shown in **bold**.

## Inspect the bilge
- [ ] **Bilge dry, or note the water level** and whether it's rising
- [ ] Identify any water: **taste/smell — salt (ingress) vs fresh (tank/rain)** vs oily
- [ ] Check no debris that could block a pump strum/strainer

## Test the pumps
- [ ] **Electric pump: run it and confirm it moves water and shuts off**
- [ ] Test the **float switch** (lift it) — confirm auto operation
- [ ] **Manual pump: confirm it primes and pumps; handle present and accessible**
- [ ] Check discharge runs freely overboard (outlet clear)

## Confirm
- [ ] Reset switches to normal (auto)
- [ ] Note anything needing attention

## Notes & gotchas
- A pump you've never tested is a pump you can't trust in a flood — test regularly.
- Rising **salt** water between checks means ingress; find the source (see flooding).
