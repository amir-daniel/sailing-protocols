# Guest / Charter Handover

- **Category:** Supplementary
- **Style:** READ–DO
- **When to use:** Taking over a charter yacht, or handing your boat to others.
- **Crew:** Skipper + the handover party.

> Killer items are shown in **bold**.

## Documentation & check-in
- [ ] Boat papers, insurance, radio licence, ship's manuals located
- [ ] Inventory checked; note existing damage **before** departure (photos)
- [ ] Emergency contacts and base/charter company procedures noted

## Safety brief (see also crew briefing)
- [ ] **Location & use of: lifejackets, harnesses, liferaft, flares, fire gear, first-aid**
- [ ] **Seacocks, bilge pumps, engine stop, gas shut-off locations**
- [ ] VHF operation and **how to send a DSC distress / Mayday**
- [ ] MOB plan and recovery gear/method for this boat

## Systems walkthrough
- [ ] Engine: start/stop, fuel, cooling-water telltale, battery switches
- [ ] Water/gas/heads operation; tank capacities and gauges
- [ ] Electrical panel, charging, and shore power
- [ ] Rig, reefing system, and sail handling specifics for this boat
- [ ] Anchor windlass operation and ground tackle

## Handover admin
- [ ] Note quirks: prop walk, how she heaves-to, docking tips
- [ ] Agree fuel/water/pump-out return condition
- [ ] Confirm cruising area limits and reporting requirements

## Notes & gotchas
- Every boat is different — never assume systems match your own; walk through them physically.
- Photograph existing damage at handover to avoid disputes later.
