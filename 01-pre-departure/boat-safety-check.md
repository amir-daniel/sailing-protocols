# Boat & Safety Check

- **Category:** Pre-departure
- **Style:** DO–CONFIRM
- **When to use:** Before leaving the dock/mooring, every time.
- **Crew:** Skipper; delegate sections to crew.

> Killer items are shown in **bold**.

## Below the waterline / engine
- [ ] **Seacocks in correct position; no leaks at through-hulls**
- [ ] Bilge dry / bilge pumps working (manual + electric)
- [ ] Engine oil & coolant levels checked
- [ ] Fuel level adequate for passage + reserve
- [ ] **Raw-water intake open; cooling water flow confirmed after start**
- [ ] Stern gland / stuffing box OK

## Deck & rig
- [ ] Rig visually checked (shrouds, stays, spreaders, fittings)
- [ ] Halyards & sheets run correctly, no chafe, not fouled
- [ ] Reefing gear ready to run
- [ ] Winch handles aboard and stowed
- [ ] Anchor & rode ready, secured but accessible

## Safety equipment
- [ ] **Lifejackets fitted, correct size, gas/light OK; harnesses & tethers ready**
- [ ] Jackstays rigged for offshore/night/heavy weather
- [ ] Liferaft in date and secured; grab bag ready (offshore)
- [ ] Flares in date; location known
- [ ] Fire extinguishers & fire blanket in place and in date
- [ ] First-aid kit aboard and accessible
- [ ] Danbuoy / horseshoe / MOB gear rigged and ready

## Electronics & comms
- [ ] VHF working; **DSC/MMSI programmed; radio check done**
- [ ] Navigation lights working
- [ ] Chartplotter / GPS on and functioning
- [ ] EPIRB / PLB registered, in date, accessible
- [ ] Phone charged; backup navigation available

## Notes & gotchas
- Confirm cooling-water telltale *after* the engine starts, not before.
- If any killer item fails, do not depart until resolved.
