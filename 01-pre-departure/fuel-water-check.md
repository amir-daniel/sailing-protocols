# Fuel & Water Check

- **Category:** Pre-departure
- **Style:** DO–CONFIRM
- **When to use:** Before departure, especially before a passage.
- **Crew:** Skipper or delegated.

> Killer items are shown in **bold**.

## Fuel
- [ ] **Confirm tank level is adequate for the passage plus a reserve** (motor allowance for calms)
- [ ] Calculate range under power vs. distance and likely wind
- [ ] Top up if needed; note where fuel is available en route
- [ ] Check for water/diesel bug in the pre-filter; drain if present
- [ ] Spare fuel filter and impeller aboard

## Fresh water
- [ ] **Confirm water tanks are adequate for crew for the passage duration**
- [ ] Top up tanks; check the pump works and no leaks
- [ ] Carry emergency drinking water separately (in the grab bag too)

## Gas (cooking)
- [ ] Confirm at least one full/adequate gas bottle
- [ ] Spare bottle secured and connected/ready
- [ ] No smell of gas; solenoid and bottle valve working

## Notes & gotchas
- Plan fuel for windless motoring, not just the rhumb-line distance.
- Ration/monitor water from day one on longer passages.
