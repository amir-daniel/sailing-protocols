# Crew Briefing

- **Category:** Pre-departure
- **Style:** READ–DO
- **When to use:** Before departure, especially with new/guest crew.
- **Crew:** Skipper briefs all aboard.

> Killer items are shown in **bold**.

## Safety orientation
- [ ] **Location & use of lifejackets and harnesses**
- [ ] **Location of liferaft, flares, fire extinguishers, first-aid kit**
- [ ] Seacock, bilge pump, and engine-stop locations
- [ ] How to use the VHF; **how to send a DSC distress / Mayday**
- [ ] Gas and stove safety

## Man-overboard plan
- [ ] "If someone goes over": shout, point, throw, press MOB button
- [ ] Who does what; recovery method aboard this boat

## Onboard rules
- [ ] "One hand for the boat"; clip-on policy (night/heavy weather/foredeck)
- [ ] Heads (toilet) operation and rules
- [ ] Seasickness: report early; medication options
- [ ] Watch system and expectations

## Roles & comms
- [ ] Assign roles for departure (lines, fenders, helm)
- [ ] Hand signals when engine noise/wind prevents speech
- [ ] Encourage questions; "if in doubt, ask"

## Notes & gotchas
- Keep it brief but hit every killer item — guests rarely remember it all,
  so reinforce during the sail.
