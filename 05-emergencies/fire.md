# Fire

- **Category:** Emergencies
- **Style:** READ–DO
- **When to use:** Any fire or smoke aboard.
- **Crew:** All hands.

> Killer items are shown in **bold**. Act fast; fire doubles quickly.

## Immediate
1. [ ] **Shout "FIRE" and locate the source**
2. [ ] **Cut off the fuel/energy source** to the fire:
   - Galley/gas: turn off gas at the bottle and the solenoid
   - Engine: stop engine, shut off fuel
   - Electrical: switch off the battery isolator / offending circuit
3. [ ] Get all non-essential crew into lifejackets, up on deck, upwind

## Fight
- [ ] Aim extinguisher at the **base** of the flames, sweep
- [ ] Galley/cooking fire: smother with a fire blanket
- [ ] **Engine fire: use the fixed system or the port — do NOT open the engine box wide** (feeds oxygen)
- [ ] Close hatches/vents to starve the fire of air where possible

## If not controlled
- [ ] **Send DSC distress + Mayday** with position and nature (fire)
- [ ] Prepare liferaft and grab bag; ready to abandon (see abandon-ship)
- [ ] Fight from a position that keeps your escape route open

## After
- [ ] Ventilate; watch for reignition
- [ ] Check for hull/structural damage and casualties
- [ ] Treat burns; seek medical advice
- [ ] Log the event

## Notes & gotchas
- Removing oxygen and fuel matters more than the extinguisher itself.
- Never open an engine compartment fully to fight a fire — use the fire port.
