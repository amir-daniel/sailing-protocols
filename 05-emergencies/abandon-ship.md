# Abandon Ship

- **Category:** Emergencies
- **Style:** READ–DO
- **When to use:** LAST RESORT — only when the boat is definitely being lost.
- **Crew:** All hands.

> Killer items are shown in **bold**. **Step UP into the liferaft** — never leave
> the boat until you have to.

## Decision
- [ ] **Only abandon when the yacht can no longer support you** (sinking/on fire/breaking up)
- [ ] The yacht is almost always a better liferaft than the raft — be sure

## Alert
1. [ ] **Send Mayday: DSC distress + voice**, position, POB, nature
2. [ ] Activate **EPIRB**; take the **PLB** on your person

## Prepare (if time allows)
- [ ] Everyone in lifejackets **and thermal protection / drysuits**
- [ ] Grab bag: water, flares, handheld VHF, PLB, first-aid, torch, warm gear
- [ ] Take extra water and any additional flares
- [ ] Deploy the liferaft **attached to the boat by its painter**, to leeward

## Board
- [ ] **Board directly / step up — stay as dry as possible**
- [ ] Get everyone in; account for all POB
- [ ] Once all aboard, **cut the painter** to clear a sinking vessel
- [ ] Stream the drogue; close the canopy; take anti-seasickness tablets

## Survive
- [ ] Priorities: **Protection, Location, Water, Food** (in that order)
- [ ] Keep watch; conserve flares for when rescue is near
- [ ] Ration water; keep the raft dry and everyone warm; maintain morale

## Notes & gotchas
- Clip the grab bag and raft painter so nothing is lost in the scramble.
- Do not inflate lifejackets *inside* the raft; you'll take up space and risk snagging.
