# Sound Signals (COLREGs)

- **Category:** COLREGs quick-reference
- **Style:** Reference
- **When to use:** In sight of another vessel (manoeuvring) and in restricted visibility.

> Memory aid only — see Rules 32–37. **·** = short blast (~1s), **▬** = long blast (4–6s).

## Manoeuvring & warning signals (Rule 34) — vessels in sight
| Signal | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **·** (one short) | "I am altering course **to starboard**" |
| **· ·** (two short) | "I am altering course **to port**" |
| **· · ·** (three short) | "I am **operating astern** propulsion" |
| **· · · · ·** (five or more short/rapid) | **Danger / "I doubt you are taking enough action"** |

### Overtaking in a narrow channel (Rule 34)
| Signal | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **▬ ▬ ·** | "I intend to overtake **on your starboard side**" |
| **▬ ▬ · ·** | "I intend to overtake **on your port side**" |
| **▬ · ▬ ·** | (Overtaken vessel) "**Agreed**" |

### Blind bend
| Signal | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **▬** (one prolonged) | Approaching a **blind bend / obstruction**; answered by any vessel beyond |

## Restricted visibility (Rule 35) — sounded at intervals (≤2 min)
| Vessel | Signal |
| --- | --- |
| **Power-driven, making way** | **▬** (one prolonged, every ≤2 min) |
| **Power-driven, underway but stopped** | **▬ ▬** (two prolonged) |
| **Sailing / fishing / RAM / NUC / constrained / towing** | **▬ · ·** (one prolonged + two short) |
| **At anchor** | Rapid ringing of a **bell** ~5s every ≤1 min (may add **· ▬ ·**) |
| **Aground** | Bell as anchor, **plus 3 separate bell strokes** before & after |

## Notes & gotchas
- **Five short blasts = "I don't understand your intentions / danger"** — use it early
  when another vessel isn't giving way.
- A sailing vessel in fog sounds **one long + two short** (same as several special vessels).
