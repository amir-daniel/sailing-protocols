# Fog / Restricted Visibility

- **Category:** Navigation & watch
- **Style:** READ–DO
- **When to use:** Visibility drops (fog, heavy rain, snow).
- **Crew:** All hands alert.

> Killer items are shown in **bold**. Follow COLREGs for restricted visibility.

## Immediate
1. [ ] **Fix and log your position now** while you still can
2. [ ] **Switch on navigation lights**
3. [ ] Post extra lookouts — **listen as well as look**
4. [ ] Reduce to a **safe speed** for the visibility

## Safety measures
- [ ] Sound the correct fog signal at intervals (under 2 min):
  - Sailing: one long + two short blasts
  - Power-driving: one long blast
- [ ] **Get all crew into lifejackets**
- [ ] Consider hoisting a radar reflector (if not fixed)
- [ ] Use radar/AIS to detect traffic; monitor CPA/TCPA
- [ ] Monitor VHF Ch 16

## Navigation decisions
- [ ] **Consider leaving/avoiding shipping lanes; head for shallow water off traffic**
- [ ] Navigate carefully with GPS + DR; watch depth as a cross-check
- [ ] If near hazards and unsure, consider anchoring in safe, shallow water

## Notes & gotchas
- Don't rely solely on radar/AIS — small craft may not show; keep human lookouts.
- Slow down: the ability to stop within the visibility is the whole point.
