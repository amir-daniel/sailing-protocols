# Watch Handover

- **Category:** Navigation & watch
- **Style:** DO–CONFIRM
- **When to use:** Every change of watch on passage.
- **Crew:** Off-going and on-coming watch.

> Killer items are shown in **bold**. Never leave the deck unattended during handover.

## Brief the new watch
- [ ] **Current position, course, and speed (COG/SOG)**
- [ ] Sail plan and any planned changes; expected wind
- [ ] **Traffic: all vessels in sight, their bearing/CPA, and intentions**
- [ ] Hazards ahead: waypoints, tide gates, marks, shipping lanes
- [ ] Weather now and expected; any forecast changes
- [ ] Boat status: engine/battery, bilge, anything not 100%

## Handover admin
- [ ] Log filled in; note anything unusual
- [ ] Point out any active alarms (AIS/radar/depth)
- [ ] Confirm the standing orders (when to call the skipper)

## Confirm
- [ ] **On-coming watch is properly clothed, clipped on, and alert before you leave**
- [ ] New watch confirms they have the picture; questions answered
- [ ] Off-going watch stays until the handover is truly complete

## Notes & gotchas
- "Call the skipper if in ANY doubt" should be an explicit standing order.
- Let the new watch's eyes adjust to the dark before you go below.
